Black Gram Sweet Balls

Anjali's Fav Sweet

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Black Gram
  • 1 cup of Sugar
  • 1 cup of unsalted Ghee

Steps For Preparation

  1. Dry roast black gram in a pan
  2. Grind the roasted gram after it cools to fine powder
  3. Grinnd the sugar to fine powder
  4. Melt the ghee
  5. Add sugar, gram powder in a bowl
  6. Add ghee and make small balls

TADAA...yummy sweet balls are ready to eat
